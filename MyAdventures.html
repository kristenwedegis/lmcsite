Planes & trains & cars, oh my!

The life the Lord set out for me has been full of adventures! I have been blessed with extraordinary opportunities to explore some of God's beautiful creations and intend on visiting many more in the years to come. From local endeavors to worldwide travel, I love experiencing new cultures, engaging in my own, and educating myself on the daily adventures of others. Click on a flag on the map below to learn more about my adventures!

NORTH AMERICA

THE UNITED STATES

Growing up in Georgia, I have thoroughly explored most of the Southeastern states in the U.S. Though the summer, Georgia heat can oftentimes be debilitating, it makes for perfect beach weather - and, luckily, the state provides access to beautiful locations, such as Tybee Island, to mitigate the effects of the hot summer sun.

CANADA

My adventures in Canda include but are not limited to Toronto, Montreal, Quebec City, and Vancouver. Because Hong Kong is so far from Georgia, there is no airline that flies a direct flight. For this reason, my mother and I have explored much of Canada during multi-day layovers in the beautiful country.

ASIA

CHINA & HONG KONG

I consider Hong Kong to be my second home. From the natural landscapes to the marvelous skyscrapers that tower over the streets, the celestial city is a hub for culture, technology, and (my favorite) food.

TOKYO, JAPAN

Tokyo is an incredible city that emphasizes the digital age we live in. As a Tech student, I fully appreciated the innovations that I found there. The city is alive with people making international advancements every day.

SEOUL, SOUTH KOREA

Seoul, South Korea is full of culture, history, and creativity. The thriving city fosters growth, both economically, demographically, as well as technologically. As like ost Asian cities, Seoul is a large hub for innovation.

AUSTRALIA

I absolutely adored Australia. From hiking the beautiful, God made landscapes to touring amazing architectural landmarks like the Sydney Opera House, Australia is full of exciting adventures around every corner.
